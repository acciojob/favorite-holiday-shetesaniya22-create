Kerala


Kerala is one of India’s most beautiful holiday destinations, known for its lush green landscapes, calm backwaters, and peaceful beaches. Often called “God’s Own Country,” it is famous for its houseboat cruises, tea gardens, wildlife, rich culture, and delicious food.
