Kerala

Kerala is one of India’s most beautiful holiday destinations, known for its lush green landscapes, calm backwaters, and peaceful beaches. Often called “God’s Own Country,” it is famous for its houseboat cruises in Alleppey and Kumarakom, tea gardens in Munnar, and wildlife in Thekkady. Kerala also offers rich culture through its classical dance forms like Kathakali and traditional festivals such as Onam. Visitors can enjoy Ayurvedic treatments, delicious seafood, and stunning sunsets along the Arabian Sea. With its mix of nature, culture, and relaxation, Kerala is a perfect destination for a peaceful and refreshing vacation.

Top 5 attractions in the destination:

  1. Munnar
  2. Alleppey (Alappuzha)
  3. Wayanad
  4. Kovalam
  5. Thekkady (Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary)

Must-try foods in the destination:

  • Appam & Stew
  • Kerala Sadya (traditional vegetarian feast)
  • Puttu & Kadala Curry
  • Banana Chips (Nendran chips)
  • Payasam (traditional dessert)
